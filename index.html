Crafting Magic Since 2018

The Heart of Flo’s Passion

A small start, real struggles, and a lot of love. Scroll down to see how CraftyOne grew—step by step.

2018 • The first spark

One helium tank. One dream.

Flo started with tiny orders and big faith—learning design by doing, and fixing mistakes at midnight.

2019 • The struggle

Budget limits, high expectations

Not every client had a big budget—so we mastered smart styling: maximum impact, minimum waste.

2020 • The reset

Hard times, stronger vision

When events paused, we pivoted to DIY kits—keeping celebrations alive inside homes.

south

2021 • The breakthrough

A viral moment

One setup got shared everywhere—suddenly we were booking weeks ahead, not days.

2023 • CraftyOne standards

Premium + sustainable

We built a process: clean palettes, sturdy installs, biodegradable materials, and zero chaos on event day.

Today • The mission

Make memories feel effortless

From custom installs to quick kits—we design celebrations that look expensive, feel joyful, and stay stress-free.
